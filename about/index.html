About Me

I am Sun Yufei.

It is hard to define who I am, but in my own eyes, I am a son, a brother, a partner, an egalitarian and a political science scholar.

“As a family member”, this will always be my most important identity.

As an egalitarian, I am concerned and act on the equality of identities, but still seek in different contradictions.

As a political scientist, my research interests include political discourse, local governance in China (also focusing on the politics of Hong Kong, Macau and Taiwan) and political psychology. I specialize in using data science to study social science issues, and I am a “Github Campus Expert”. As a proponent of open science, I am dedicated to fostering an environment in which scientific research can be conducted and shared openly and equitably, thereby enabling all individuals to reap the benefits of scientific progress. To this end, I am honored to have been appointed as an Open Science Ambassador for the Center for Open Science.

As a faculty member, I work at Tsinghua University International Student and Scholar Center and Tsinghua University Library, responsible for the management of international students and the promotion of open science practices at Tsinghua University. I have worked as a practicing assistant director at the Finance Office of Ordos, Inner Mongolia as an unpaid position.
